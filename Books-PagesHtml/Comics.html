Ultimate Spider-Man

Author: Brian Michael Bendis

Genre: Superhero, Action, Adventure

Published: 2000 - 2011

Summary

Ultimate Spider-Man chronicles the adventures of a young Peter Parker as he navigates the challenges of being a superhero. This modern retelling delves into the origins of Spider-Man, his struggles with power and responsibility, and his encounters with iconic villains. Join Peter as he balances his personal life with his duty to protect New York City.

Batman

Author: Bob Kane, Bill Finger

Genre: Superhero, Crime, Detective

Published: 1939 - Present

Summary

Batman, also known as the Dark Knight, follows the story of Bruce Wayne, a billionaire playboy who, after witnessing the tragic murder of his parents, dedicates his life to fighting crime in Gotham City. Using his intellect, martial arts skills, and advanced technology, Batman combats injustice while facing a rogues' gallery of villains, including the iconic Joker, Catwoman, and more.

Deadpool

Author: Fabian Nicieza, Rob Liefeld

Genre: Superhero, Comedy, Action

Published: 1991 - Present

Summary

Deadpool, also known as the "Merc with a Mouth," chronicles the story of Wade Wilson, a former special forces operative turned mercenary. After undergoing a rogue experiment that grants him accelerated healing powers but leaves him disfigured, Deadpool adopts his alter ego, wielding his dark sense of humor, sarcasm, and exceptional combat skills. Breaking the fourth wall and taking on unconventional missions, Deadpool navigates the world of superheroes while engaging in hilarious and action-packed adventures.

Wolverine

Author: Roy Thomas, Len Wein, John Romita Sr.

Genre: Superhero, Action, Adventure

Published: 1974 - Present

Summary

Wolverine, also known as Logan or James Howlett, is a mutant possessing accelerated healing abilities and retractable adamantium claws. A member of the X-Men, he navigates the world as a gritty and fierce warrior with a mysterious past. With his iconic adamantium claws and animalistic instincts, Wolverine embarks on adventures, delving into his tumultuous history, facing enemies, and striving to protect both mutants and humanity.

Black Adam

Author: Otto Binder, C. C. Beck

Genre: Superhero, Action, Adventure

Published: 1945 - Present

Summary

Black Adam, originally a counterpart to the superhero Shazam, possesses powers derived from ancient Egyptian gods. Teth-Adam, the original Black Adam, is an anti-hero or antagonist, often opposing or collaborating with various heroes in the DC Universe. His story delves into themes of power, redemption, and the conflict between justice and vengeance.
